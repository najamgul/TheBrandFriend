ON RESTRAINT

It is in the absence of noise that the true character of an object is revealed. The design process becomes one of subtraction rather than addition.

We approach each environment not as an empty container to be filled, but as a silent field of potential. The objects placed within it must possess enough inherent gravity to anchor the space without dominating it.
